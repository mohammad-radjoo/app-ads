Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Lucky Egg ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by Radjoo Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how information is handled when you use our game.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Game progress and scores stored locally on your device
  • Anonymous crash reports and diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers for ads and analytics
  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Anonymous usage statistics

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts or photos
  • Precise location data
  • Payment information

Advertising

Lucky Egg uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Third-party advertising partners may collect advertising identifiers and anonymous device information to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

Users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland may be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown.

Third-Party Services

Lucky Egg may use third-party services including:

  • Google AdMob (advertising services)
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)

These services may collect anonymous technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Most game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store personal user information. Removing the app may delete locally stored game progress.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, including under GDPR regulations, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of locally stored data
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising
  • Request information about data processing

Children's Privacy

Lucky Egg is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Personalized advertising may be restricted for underage users where required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the revised policy.
